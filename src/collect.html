    How collect works

    Vendor management can be an overwhelmingly large task for merchants. Maintaining relationships with 100â€™s of suppliers and requesting, receiving or fetching and storing the product data and updates from each vendor, can quickly drain resources and lead to disorganization and data overload.

    Vesta provides the solution, by setting up automated workflows to collect product data from each of your vendors continuously over time.

    API connection from Vendor to you

    Vesta works with your vendors to link directly to their data source whether it be ERP or FTP.

    Not only do you get access to their entire range, all future updates and changes will flow through automatically direct to your system.

    Data uploaded to Supplier portal

    Suppliers quickly and easily upload product information including assets such as photos and videos to the Vesta SupplierPortal.

    They continue to login to this interface over time to load new products or updates.

    Scraping of the Supplier website

    If the supplier is unable to provide product data, we can set up scraping services of their website to retrieve the information needed.
