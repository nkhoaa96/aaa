    How clean works

    Merchants face the challenge of receiving vendor product data in divergent states. These differences can be both in format (such as excel, CSV, XML) but also with different names or measures. For instance weights in kg or pounds or ounces, and different names for the same thing such as sku, product or item.

    Re-working all of this data into a consistent format, ready to feed into a PIM or eCommerce platform, takes many many hours filled with frustration for a merchant to perform manually and is fraught with the potential for inaccuracies

    Vesta provides the solution, saving hours of frustration, by automating the process of transforming vendor data into an optimized state, ready to feed into your eCommerce store.

    Map and Merge the incoming data

    The different data fields are mapped and then merged to combine them into a centralized single source of truth for your product data.

    Data Transformation

    Vesta's transformation engine will translate the data received from a vendor, based on rules set up specifically for that data feed, into the standardized and optimal format for your online store.

    Transformations include actions such as conversions, adding words, joining fields or replacing words.

    Identifying Inaccurate Data

    Vesta's Validation Engine identifies errors such as incorrect formats, missing fields or duplicates.

    These errors are highlighted, ready for easy correction within the Vesta Portal.
