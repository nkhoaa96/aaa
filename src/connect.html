    Product data

    Keep the product content in your online store up to date with product changes synced directly from Vesta to your PIM or online store.

    How connect works

    Filling your online store with products is a task that can take weeks or months, especially for merchants with more than 10,000 products. Once the products are loaded, unfortunately the job doesn’t end there. With product additions and deletions, product upgrades and changes such as prices increases happening frequently — keeping you rstore updates is a a constant and time consuming task.

    Vesta provides the solution. With seamless integration with your eCommerce platform such as BigCommerce or Shopify, we can ensure the clean data flows with real time updates from Vesta directly into your PIM or eCommerce platform.

    With Vesta integrated with your PIM or eCommerce platform, you can:

    • Automatically load new products to your online store, including all of their rich product content;
    • Update existing products with new information, such as changes submitted to you from your vendor;
    • Turn product availability on and off based on pre-determined rules;
    • Adjust price and stock levels.
    Save hours spent uploading product data. Bring new products to market faster.
